# StayOS — Hotel Management System
### Executive Summary

**StayOS** is a complete digital management system for a hotel (built and demonstrated for a 30-room property). It centralises the entire operation — from the moment a guest books until check-out, invoicing and the final review — in a single, modern, secure web platform. Staff and guests use it directly in the browser, with **no software to install**. The interface is fully available in **English and Dutch**.

## What it does

**Operations**
- Central dashboard with live KPIs: occupancy, ADR, RevPAR, revenue, satisfaction.
- Visual room map (30 rooms, 3 floors) with real-time status.
- Reservations from all channels, with full status flow.
- Digital check-in / check-out with one click; rooms update automatically.
- Housekeeping board (cleaning by priority) and maintenance (issues, priorities, room blocking).
- Guest service requests, routed to the right department.

**Business**
- Billing: invoices, receipts and credit notes with automatic numbering; mark-paid; CSV export for accounting.
- Reviews: ratings by category, alerts for negative feedback, forwarding satisfied guests to Booking/Google.
- Automated messaging: editable templates (confirmation, pre-check-in, welcome, mid-stay, check-out, thank-you + review) by email/WhatsApp.
- Guest CRM: profiles, preferences, history, spend and segmentation (VIP, returning, company, occasional).

**Guest experience**
- A guest portal accessed by link or QR code — no app. Guests view their booking, request services (cleaning, room service, spa, transport, maintenance) and contact reception. Requests flow straight into the staff dashboard.

**Channels & control**
- Booking.com / OTA synchronisation with **automatic overbooking prevention**.
- Exportable reports (CSV).
- User roles and permissions, plus an activity log for full traceability.
- Settings screen to manage the hotel details and users without touching code.

## The guest journey
Booking → pre-check-in (link sent before arrival) → arrival & room assignment → stay & service requests → check-out → invoice → review request.

## Benefits
- Less manual work and fewer errors (automation across the operation).
- A better, faster guest experience.
- Full, real-time control of the hotel.
- One system instead of many disconnected tools.

## Status
A **working system** has been delivered across four phases (operations, guest flow, business & communication, channels & production readiness) and is running as a live demo. It is ready for a pilot.

## Next steps for full production

- Certified fiscal invoicing for the country of operation.
- Persistent database with automatic backups; PDF/Excel reports.
